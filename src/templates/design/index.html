Beginner to intermediate

“Hands on Design thinking”

Get to know the methodology and how to apply that to working tasks. We will go through 5 steps of Design Thinking Empathize - Define - Ideate - Prototype - Test and present our works.

In Part Two of the Design Track, you’ll explore the methodology of design thinking with Alina. This covers 5 steps: Empathy, Define, Ideate, Prototype and Test. The hands on design thinking workshop will leave you with a finished product.

Requirements:

  1. Bring your laptop with Powerpoint/Keynote/Figma and creativity!
  2. Paper, pens-pencils
  3. No knowledge pre-required

Duration 15:00 - 17:00

Agenda:

  1. Intro and overview | Introduce the goals and plan for the workshop, conduct exercises and set the creativity mood!
  2. Design thinking Concept | An introduction and overview to the Design Thinking approach, introduce the challenge for the workshop
  3. Empathy | Practice interviewing skills. On this step we are going to share insights and create Empathy Map
  4. Define | Define phase concepts and frame problem to be able to develop problem-statements
  5. Ideation | In-depth session on Ideation phase. Brainstorming session related to our mission, synthesis of generated ideas
  6. Prototype/testing | On this step we are going to take the best ideas and make into something tangible to share, review with potential end-users for feedback, revise and iterate as needed
  7. Presentations | Share potential solutions and discuss findings, review presentation and storytelling skills, create final presentations
  8. Wrap-up | Reflect on what we have learned: discuss plans for using Design Thinking at work
